Our Services

Services provided by Powercare

Powercare helps the energy consumers through the life cycle of a Solar power plant right from the feasibility and design to operations and maintenance through their project life. Our Target Market is Commercial & Industrial Power Consumers and Project Developers & Investors.

Solar EPC

Powercare has an in-house end-to-end capability to build a customized Solar powered plant. Our team is seasoned experts with experience in successfully handling projects involving every type of roof.

Solar O&M

We have a dedicated Operations & Maintenance team for all our projects and also managing the Solar assets of leading Project developers & Investors. The team monitors and services the Solar assets 24×7 leading to optimal power generation and maximum plant uptime.

Solar BOOT

We invest, build and operate Solar plants for Commercial and Industrial entities while they pay only for the energy used. A suitable Power Purchase Agreement is signed to bring this into effect.
